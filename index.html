Migration to the Cardano Network

We are delighted to announce a fundamental step for the Atomico3 ecosystem. In collaboration with our technology partner, ZenGate Global, we have decided to migrate the current Atomico3 contract from the Polygon network to the Cardano network.
ZenGate Global is at the forefront of innovation in Cardano, and this move allows us to leverage its advanced capabilities to introduce a financing mechanism for mining assets using algorithmic token technology, backed by lithium reserves.
Existing Atomico3 holders will retain their stake in the new Cardano-based contract. This contract is designed to tokenize mining assets effectively, ensuring that the token issuance is backed by these assets while operating on a highly secure and transparent network that safeguards the interests of the industry.

About Atomico3

We are a legally established company in the Republic of Paraguay, dedicated to the development and optimization of mining activities, particularly lithium mining.
We bring to the mining industry the tool of tokenization (of its certified lithium reserves), aiming to innovate and revolutionize the electromobility industry worldwide. Through the conversion of these certified lithium reserves into digital tokens, we facilitate funding for the industry’s operations. Contributing to the comprehensive development of their activities and transitioning to a more sustainable future.
